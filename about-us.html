Meet the Triple Ds

We are the Triple Ds

Our Names are Dani, Deeg and Donna.

Dani - Singer Extrodinaire

Dani is the Triple Ds singer. She likes to sing all the time - not just when she is playing with the Triple Ds! Her mum and dad say she learned to sing before she learned to talk!

Deeg - If its got strings, she'll play it!

Acoustic guitar? Electric lead guitar? Ukelele? Harp? Deeg can play them all. She started off by making homemade guitars from carboard boxes and elastic bands till her uncle took pity on her and brought her a Spanish Guitar for Christmas. After a few lessons at school she was flying!

Donna - One Woman Rythm Section

Donna has had music in her blood from the day she was born. Both her mum and dad play instruments and got her her first drum kit when she was only five years old, which led to trouble with the neighbours. On some songs she will play the bass guitar and on others she will play the drums. She loves playing in the Tr Ds but hates carrying her drumkit around.

Triple Ds - Buy Our New CD!

Great if you're learning to play!

Are your kids learning to play musical instruments? Then why not order this great CD of Triple Ds favourite songs to sing along to! As well as having all the complete songs, the CD includes versions of the songs with each instrument missing - so you can play along with your own instrument. Not only that, but the solo parts and the music are also included so you can copy what to play.
